About

Hello! I am Utkarsh, a Singer, Songwriter, and Composer from India who goes by @devutkarsh on the interwebz. A software techie & introverted human hustling towards his passion for music, who is on a mission to talk about all the unsaid emotions through his melodies.

I started my musical journey along with my Music Producer and friend, Ankit Godle. We both collaborated under the production studio Soul Raaga to never stop again!
