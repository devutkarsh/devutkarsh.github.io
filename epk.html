Biography

Meet Dev Utkarsh, a singer, songwriter, and composer hailing from Bengaluru, India focuses on writing what mostly are the unsaid emotions. His songwriting style goes back to personal issues and experiences that are mostly reflected by the introvered humans of the society. Born and bought up in Lucknow, though having roots from a small village in Uttar Pradesh his life was highly influenced by folk music and radio. Early from the days of listening different music to writing shayaris, Dev started learning musicial instruements at the age of 16, which eventually opened up his emotions to write as felt.

He balances his life as a software techie while pursuing his true passion for music. Dev Utkarsh is on a mission to convey unspoken emotions through the medium of his melodies.
